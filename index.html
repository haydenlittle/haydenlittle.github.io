Who I Am

Salve! I’m Johnny Rabe, and I’m running to be your national Parliamentarian.

Having studied Latin since 3rd grade, I really fell in love with the classics after reading the Percy Jackson series and many other books by Rick Riordan.



I love studying not only the Latin language but also classical culture, mythology, art, and architecture. A coast-to-coast hike of Hadrian’s wall last summer with my brother provided great opportunities for exploring Roman architecture. I also love musical theatre and have been in many shows, from school performances to Broadway productions.

I’ve served as President of my school’s JCL chapter and the Parliamentarian of the Illinois JCL.

Why I Should Be Your Parliamentarian



I have the experience, ideas, and enthusiasm to be a top-notch NJCL Parliamentarian.

My goals as NJCL Parliamentarian would include:

  • Encouraging more people to run for national office
    • I would build upon the Campaign Corner and expand its distribution.
  • Ensuring that delegates have widespread access to candidates
    • Candidates Lookbook: At the conclusion of Nom Com, I would create a lookbook of candidate photos and profiles and make it available to delegates via social media and state fellowships.
    • Candidates Connect: I would provide the capability for delegates to ask questions of candidates via social media.

I have experience as a state-level Parliamentarian and extensive experience with Robert’s Rules of Order through both the Illinois JCL and Model UN.

And I am head over heels in love with the JCL!
