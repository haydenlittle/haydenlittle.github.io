Alumni

Delta Tau Delta at Stanford has a distinguished roster of over 1,600 alumni, and nationally Delta Tau Delta has an alumni roster of over 170,000. Most of our brothers - graduated or still active - will tell you that at nearly every job or opportunity they have partaken in outside of the classroom they have met someone who is a Delt.
