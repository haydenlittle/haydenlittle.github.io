Social

It's no secret that Stanford's academics are pretty tough. The goal of our social programming is not meant to be a distraction from the importance of academics, but instead a method to balance a healthy social life that complements a great student. While we will perhaps never again in our lives be surrounded by greater academic resources, we will also perhaps never again in our lives be surrounded by such interesting, diverse, and passionate students - we place a great deal of importance on soaking up as much of that side of Stanford as possible as well!

Delta Tau Delta hosts tailgates, all-campus parties, mixers, formals, and more. As the school year starts winding up, be sure to check below to see what we've got coming for you soon!
