Involvement

Delta Tau Delta is one of the most involved greek life organizations on campus. We make it a point to expand our footprint on the Stanford campus far beyond our fraternity. We each participate in our own activities on campus, but there are a few that we enjoy together. We have the largest greek presence in many organizations, among them are Stanford Rugby and BASES.

Rugby

Our chapter has six members of the Stanford Rugby Team and many more Stanford Rugby Alumni. Rugby is a grueling sport that requires mental toughness, impeccable fitness, and incredible perserverance. One of the highlights of our winter quarter is supporting our brothers on the team and we enjoy the skills they take off the field.

BASES

The Business Assosciation of Stanford Entrepreneurial Students (BASES) is one of the premiere student-run entrepreneurship organizations in the world. Its primary mission is to promote entrepreneurship education at Stanford University, and we have 5 brothers who are officers in the organization.
